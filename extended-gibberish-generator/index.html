Extended Gibberish Generator

This is a mod of the gibberish generator from Keith's Think Zone that removes arbitrary restrictions and adds some extra capabilities. For instance, the level and length of the gibberish can now be made arbitrary, and there is also an option to create progressive gibberish.

Bear in mind that the orthography of the gibberish resembles a stroke rather than jibber jabber. Feel free to farm karma at r/IHadAStroke or get a rise out of your friends with your generated text.
